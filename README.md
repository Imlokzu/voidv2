# Void Music App

> A darker, cleaner, more premium YouTube Music client.

Base: [SimpMusic](https://github.com/maxrave-dev/SimpMusic)

---

## Product

**Void** lets you import any song, make your version, and listen without algorithmic trash.

### Pillars

1. **Import Your Music** — Spotify, SoundCloud, Telegram, YouTube
2. **My Version** — Per-song audio presets (slowed, bass, reverb)
3. **No Random Tracks** — Only play what you actually saved
4. **Animated Music UI** — Artist pages that feel alive
5. **Better Library** — Folders, search, sorting, duplicate finder
6. **Void-native design** — Dark glass, purple accent, portal branding
